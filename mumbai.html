Visit Mumbai

Enjoy your stay in the city of dreams, one of the biggest cities of the world

Visit the Gateway of India

Built in the 20th century for welcoming the King of England. Experience the natural harbour of Mumbai and various ferry rides around the harbour.

Sit at Marine Drive

Sit close to the sea and experience its vastness. Also known as Queen's Necklace due to its semi circular shape.

Go for a jungle safari

In the top of the city exists a sanctuary knowns as Sanjay Gandhi National Park, visit it to see various species of animals and the major attraction of Great Bengal Tiger.
